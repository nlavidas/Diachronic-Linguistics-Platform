## üìö **Module Structure (8 Weeks, 6 ECTS)**

### **Week 1-2: Foundations**
- Introduction to NLP for ancient languages
- TEI XML and corpus standards
- Python for linguists (Jupyter notebooks)

### **Week 3-4: Annotation**
- PROIEL treebank methodology
- Penn-Helsinki phrase structures
- Hands-on: Annotate 500 tokens (Greek/Latin)

### **Week 5-6: Analysis**
- Statistical methods for language change
- Visualizing diachronic trends
- Case study: SOV ‚Üí SVO in Greek

### **Week 7-8: Capstone**
- Student projects (1,000 token annotation)
- Peer review and quality control
- Presentation and defense
